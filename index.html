About Me

Hello, I am Jayant!

Greetings! I'm Jayant Kuppuswamy, a 17-year-old senior currently attending Wayland High School. My journey began in Massachusetts, where I was born, but took a detour to Mumbai, India, before circling back to Wayland, where I now call home. As I prepare for the next chapter of my academic journey, I am eagerly anticipating my enrollment at George Washington University in the fall of 2024, where I plan to pursue a major in international relations. My time in India sparked a deep-seated interest in global affairs and cultural exchange, driving my desire to understand and contribute to the complexities of our interconnected world. Academically, my passions lie in history, where I find myself engrossed in the narratives of the past, uncovering the lessons that shape our present and future. However, my enthusiasm wanes when confronted with the intricacies of math and science, areas where I struggle to find my footing despite my best efforts. Outside the classroom, I find solace and excitement in the world of sports, despite not being the most athletically inclined individual. Whether cheering from the sidelines or catching the game on TV, there's an undeniable thrill in the camaraderie and competition that sports bring. As I embark on this new adventure, I am filled with a sense of anticipation and eagerness for the opportunities and challenges that await me. I am confident that my experiences, both in and out of the classroom, have equipped me with the skills and perspective necessary to thrive at George Washington University and beyond.
